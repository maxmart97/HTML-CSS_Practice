CSS3: HSL & HSLA

This paragraph uses HSL colors. H is hue, the color, expressed as a number between 0 and 360 degrees. S is saturation, the amount of gray in a color, expressed as a percentage. L is lightness, the amount of white or black in a color, expressed as a percentage.

This paragraph uses HSLA colors. Everything is the same as HSL with the added ability to specify alpha, the opacity.
